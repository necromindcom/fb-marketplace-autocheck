Description of Service

Marketup is a free browser extension that helps users efficiently select Facebook Marketplace groups for their listings by providing keyword-based filtering and automated selection.

Use of the Extension

Permitted Use

You may use the Extension to:

  • Automate the selection of Facebook Marketplace groups based on keyword filters
  • Save and manage filter presets for different types of listings
  • Improve your efficiency when posting to multiple Facebook groups
