Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What does this extension do?

Marketup automatically selects up to 20 Facebook groups for your Marketplace listing based on keyword filters you set. Instead of manually scrolling through hundreds of groups, the extension finds and checks the most relevant ones in seconds.

How much does it cost?

Marketup costs just $1.19 per month. This affordable subscription gives you unlimited access to all features including smart filtering, unlimited presets, and priority support. Cancel anytime with no questions asked.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely! All processing happens locally in your browser. The extension only interacts with Facebook's page that's already open. No data is sent to external servers, and your listings remain completely private.
