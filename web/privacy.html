Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Marketup ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We DON'T Collect

Marketup does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do NOT collect your Facebook login credentials
  • We do NOT track your browsing history
  • We do NOT collect information about your listings
  • We do NOT send any data to external servers
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do NOT share any information with third parties

How the Extension Works

Marketup operates entirely within your browser:

  • Local Processing: All keyword filtering and group selection happens locally on your computer
  • Local Storage: Your saved presets and widget preferences are stored only in your browser's local storage using Chrome's storage API
  • No External Communication: The extension does not make any network requests or communicate with external servers
  • Facebook Only: The extension only interacts with Facebook Marketplace pages that are already open in your browser

Permissions Explained

The extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

activeTab & scripting

These permissions allow the extension to read and interact with the Facebook Marketplace group selection page. This is necessary to find checkboxes, read group names, and automatically select/deselect groups based on your filters.

storage

This permission allows the extension to save your filter presets and widget preferences locally in your browser. No data leaves your computer.

host_permissions (facebook.com)

This limits the extension to only work on Facebook.com pages. The extension cannot access any other websites you visit.

Data Storage

The only data stored by the extension is:

  • Filter Presets: Your saved keyword combinations (e.g., "gaming, xbox")
  • Widget Position: The location where you last placed the floating widget
  • Widget State: Whether the widget was collapsed or expanded

All this data is stored locally using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API and never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Marketup does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

The extension does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13 years of age. The extension is designed for Facebook Marketplace users, who must be at least 18 years old per Facebook's terms.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
